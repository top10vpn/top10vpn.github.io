What are the main reasons for getting a VPN service?


1. Privacy and Anonymity

There is no better way to keep your surfing private then using a VPN. The second you connect to a VPN's server, your Internet Service Provider will not be able to track which websites you visit, thereby keeping your browsing habits as they should be - Private!

2. Improved Security

Using a VPN service adds a layer of protection to your Internet connection. This increased encryption means you are safer from hackers. Whenever you make a transaction online, or when you need to send or receive sensitive information, a VPN will enhance your security.

3. Bypassing geo-restrictions

If you want to unlock restricted web content and gain access to any website, online service or app, get a VPN. Wanna watch US Netflix, BBC or Hulu? No matter where you are in the world, with a VPN you can just change your IP to any country, thereby bypassing any restriction quickly and easily.

How does a VPN work?


A VPN quite simply creates a secure (encrypted) internet connection – also known as a “ VPN tunnel” – between your PC or mobile device and the website or app you want to visit.

Once you are connected via a VPN software, your regular Internet connection will be redirected to a secure server.

This also means that your IP address will now be different, so you will essentially be surfing from a hidden location, rather than the one you live in. The VPN software allows you to choose which country to connect from, so if you live in Mexico for example, and you connect to a US server, your IP will now be completely American.
