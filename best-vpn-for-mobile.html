Do you need a VPN for your mobile device?


As with desktop computers, the issue of secure data privacy still remains with mobile operating systems - maybe even more so. iPhone and android users are as susceptible to issues like government surveillance and attempts by hackers to steal personal information as they are on their PCs or Macs.

Unencrypted and unprotected Internet usage can expose your personal data to ISPs, or - more worryingly - to cybercriminals. The latter threat is especially relevant for mobile device use, as these more often connect to public wi-fi hotspots. These are some of the most important scenarios that make a VPN such a useful tool for highly portable devices.

Don't think that a VPN is only relevant for illegal activities. If you frequently travel or connect to public WIFI networks in restaurants or cafes, and you don’t want some hacker going through your private emails, or your banking information ending up on the dark web, get a VPN app for your mobile device.
