Why should I get a VPN for my Mac?


Even though Macs are generally safer than Windows PCs, there are various reasons why downloading and installing a Mac VPN is becoming an ever more popular thing to do with an Apple computer as we head into 2020, including:

- As an added security layer from cyber attacks
- To enjoy unrestricted access to Netflix, iPlayer & HULU from wherever you are in the world
- To browse the web with fast speeds
- The ability to safely and anonymously download torrents and P2P files

As the popularity of Apple’s desktop computers and MacBooks grows, they are being increasingly targeted by cybercriminals, so it never pays to let your guard down. Your Apple computer does not enjoy special protection from data-hungry spies, advertisers, and ISPs. That's why virtual private networks, or VPNs, are so important, even for Mac users.

While you're thinking about privacy for your Mac, you should also consider security. If you're not protected yet, you ought to read our roundup of the best Mac antivirus software.

Important features to look out for...


Although many providers claim to be compatible with Mac, finding a truly Mac-Friendly option is not always easy. Make sure that the VPN you choose for your Mac has a pre-configured client for its service. Decent iOS support is also a welcome feature, if you love the Apple ecosystem and have an iPhone as well as a Mac. All of our top picks above offer these and many more features, making them the ideal choice for fans of Apple products.

Should I get a free Mac VPN?


While there are fewer decent free VPNs available for Mac than for windows, it is always better to install a free one than not to use one at all.

Like everything in life, you get what you pay for and if you want to surf the web without any concerns for you privacy, we highly recommend you spend a few bucks on getting the best VPN for your Mac.

The paid versions typically offer monthly subscriptions that cost less than $5, making this an effective and relatively cheap added layer of security for you and your Apple devices.
