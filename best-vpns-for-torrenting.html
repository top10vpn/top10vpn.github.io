How To Choose An Optimal VPN For Torrenting


While torrenting by itself isn’t against the law, downloading copyrighted materials may get you into legal trouble. And although VPNs do not, by any means, endorse this illegal practice, they do offer people a safety net against potential fines and law suits. By channeling your traffic through a secure and hidden Internet connection, a VPN makes it impossible for anyone other than yourself to see what you do online.

There are many VPN services out there. When choosing an optimal VPN for torrenting, make sure it offers special servers optimized for P2P traffic, as well as unlimited bandwidth and top-notch security features like DNS leak protection, and a kill-switch.
